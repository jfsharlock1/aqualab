How the Scanner Works

This page is for explaining the science: light, color sensors, and how our app turns RGB values into pool-chemistry numbers.

  • Camera first measures raw RGB colors from each pad.
  • We divide by a white-balance value so different lighting looks consistent.
  • For each pad, we compare the measured color to the brand’s color chart. The closest match gives us the ppm value.
  • Those ppm values plus the pool gallon estimate feed into standard pool-service formulas to calculate how much chemical to add.
